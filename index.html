Hello!

I'm a master's student in the Department of Geography at Pennsylvania State University studying Geographic Information Science. My research looks at crowdsourcing techniques and machine learning techniques to improve environmental datasets. I'm interested in data mining, crowdsourcing methodologies, spatial analysis, and digital humanities. I contribute to the Human Factors in GIScience Lab, directed by Dr. Alexander Klippel, and was a past contributor to the Peter R. Gould Center directed by Dr. Cynthia Brewer.

Here's a link to my CV

Research

I am generally interested in the comparison between the performance of machine learning classifiers and human classifiers in the context of environmental datasets. My current major research is focused on the cognitive aspects of landscape perception and ways to increase the effectiveness of human land cover classification. The applications for this research have impacts in land cover dataset creation, crowdsourcing and machine learning techniques for environmental classification, and citizen science.
