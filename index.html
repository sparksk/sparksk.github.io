I'm a geospatial information scientist interested in data mining, machine learning, crowdsourcing, and spatial analysis. I currently work as a research intern at Oak Ridge National Laboratory.

I have a master's degree in geographic information science from the Department of Geography at Penn State University. My research looked at crowdsourcing techniques and machine learning techniques to improve land cover datasets. During my time at Penn State (2009 - 2015) I worked with Dr. Alexander Klippel and Dr. Cynthia Brewer.
